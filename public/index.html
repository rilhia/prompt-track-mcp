Load a track

The player ships empty, no track is included. A track is one JSON file that overlays timing, cues and an AI persona onto YouTube videos. Load one from a URL or a local file to begin, or author your own and share the file so others can load it here. The videos themselves are never modified.

Companion

Materials

Notes, tasks and quizzes appear here as the track plays.

01Reuse what already exists

Take any YouTube videos and cut, splice and reorder them into something new. Build a course, a lesson, a guided watch-along or a comedy edit from footage that is already out there, without reshooting a single frame or re-hosting anything.

02Video that talks back

Your edit does not just play, it converses. Claude sits beside it through the MCP, seeing exactly where the viewer is, and answers questions, runs quizzes and explains the moment. Content that speaks to the viewer and speaks back.

03An instructor built in

Give Claude a persona and your reused footage gains a host, a tutor or a collaborator. Viewers explore any question or idea in that voice, turning passive clips into a two-way experience you author once and anyone can step into.
